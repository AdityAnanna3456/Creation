About BAC International Study Centre

Visit Bac International Study Centre

BAC International is a vibrant educational institution of higher learning. Its ideology is devoted to establishing a standard for quality instruction in order to obtain an abroad degree and satisfy the demands of a rapidly rising market economy. It recognizes Bangladesh's progress toward becoming a knowledge-based society, which necessitates the development of a highly trained and competent workforce.As a learning facilitator brand, it has a unique role in Bangladeshi education. Its programs are given in conjunction with the University of London, Leeds Trinity University, and Edexcel, UK, in an environment that encourages students to achieve success in their pursuit of international certifications and degrees as soon as they graduate from high school.

What is a Software?

Software is a set of instructions for a computer. The entire set of programs, procedures, and routines associated with the operation of a computer system is referred to as software. The phrase was coined to distinguish these instructions from the physical components of a computer system, which are known as hardware.The system software is a collection of programs that operate, control, and extend the computer's processing capabilities. The majority of system software is created by the computer makers. These software products are made up of low-level language programs that interact with hardware on a very basic level. The interaction between the hardware and the end users is provided by system software.
