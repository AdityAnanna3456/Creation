Multiple Code Lines

For multiple lines of code, use the pre element: Text in a pre element is displayed in a fixed-width font, and it preserves both spaces and line breaks.

Abbreviations

Abbreviations The abbr element is used to mark up an abbreviation or acronym: The WHO was founded in 1948. WHO keeps tell people about Covid-19.

Blockquotes

The blockquote element is used to present content from another source: For 50 years, WWF has been protecting the future of nature. The world's leading conservation organization, WWF works in 100 countries and is supported by 1.2 million members in the United States and close to 5 million globally. From WWF's website.
